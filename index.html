My Cat Profile

Shere Khan

Shere — also known as Sherecat, Ratazana, or Pitiquinho — is a male Brazilian Shorthair. He’s naughty in the cutest way: he loves playing with random objects around the house, especially cardboard boxes. He’s also very possessive of his belongings and constantly checks whether his sister is using them (they don’t get along very well). Despite his dramatic side, he’s a loving cat who demands plenty of attention and affection. He adores being petted many times throughout the day and never misses a chance to ask for cuddles.
